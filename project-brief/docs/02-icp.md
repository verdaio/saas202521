# Ideal Customer Profile & Signals

## ICP
- 50–250 employees
- Microsoft 365 (Entra ID, SharePoint, Teams) already in use
- HRIS: BambooHR, Rippling, Paylocity, or similar
- Hiring 5–20 roles/month or experiencing seasonal hiring bursts
- Complaints on Glassdoor/reviews about onboarding chaos or slow IT

## Signals to Qualify
- ≥8 open roles on careers page
- Job posts mention M365/SharePoint/Teams/Power Automate
- Recent funding, new COO/VP People in last 3–12 months
- Evidence of manual checklists or email‑driven onboarding
